Team photo assets
=================

Drop the four leadership photos in this folder using exactly these filenames:

  dong-phong.jpg    - Founder & CEO
  kim-chi.jpg       - Marketing Director
  tuan-anh.jpg      - Operations Director
  quoc-truong.jpg   - Tech & Analytics

Recommended specs:
  - Square crop (1:1), e.g. 800×800px
  - JPG or WebP, 80% quality
  - Center the face, soft neutral background
  - File size under 200KB each

Until the real photo is dropped in, the card shows a styled placeholder
with the person's initial - so the layout always looks intentional.
